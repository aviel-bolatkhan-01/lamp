Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What We Collect

When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email address. We also store your game progress (score, XP, achievements) in our database.

2. How We Use It

Your information is used solely to provide the game experience — saving your progress, displaying your name on leaderboards, and enabling multiplayer features. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for authentication and data storage, and Google/Apple for sign-in. Their privacy policies apply to their respective services.

5. Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Guest play is available without creating an account.

6. Children

The game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.
