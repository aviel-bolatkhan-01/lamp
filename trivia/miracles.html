Question 1
Who was the first person to witness Jesus after His resurrection?
  • Mary Magdalene
  • Peter
  • John
  • James
John 20:14-18 records Jesus appearing first to Mary Magdalene at the tomb.
Question 2
What was Jesus' first miracle?
  • Turning water into wine
  • Healing the blind man
  • Walking on water
  • Raising Lazarus
Jesus turned water into wine at the wedding in Cana, as recorded in John 2:1-11.
Question 3
Which prophet was fed by ravens during a drought?
  • Elijah
  • Elisha
  • Jeremiah
  • Isaiah
God commanded ravens to bring Elijah bread and meat in the morning and evening (1 Kings 17:4-6).
Question 4
How many loaves of bread did Jesus use to feed the 5,000?
  • 5
  • 7
  • 2
  • 12
Jesus used five loaves and two fish to feed the 5,000 (Matthew 14:17).
Question 5
What miracle occurred when Peter and John were entering the temple through the 'Beautiful Gate'?
  • A lame man was healed
  • A blind man saw
  • A dead man was raised
  • A demon was cast out
Peter said, 'Silver or gold I do not have, but what I do have I give you,' and the man was healed (Acts 3:1-10).
Question 6
Who was raised from the dead after being dead for four days?
  • Lazarus
  • Jairus' daughter
  • The widow's son
  • Tabitha
Jesus called Lazarus from the tomb four days after his death (John 11:1-44).
Question 7
Which miracle involves a coin found in a fish's mouth?
  • Paying the temple tax
  • Feeding the 4,000
  • The miraculous catch of fish
  • Jesus' first miracle
Jesus told Peter to find a four-drachma coin in the first fish he caught to pay their taxes (Matthew 17:24-27).
Question 8
Who touched the hem of Jesus' garment and was healed?
  • A woman with a discharge of blood
  • Mary Magdalene
  • The daughter of Jairus
  • The Samaritan woman
She had suffered for 12 years and was healed by touching His cloak (Matthew 9:20-22).
Question 9
What happened to the sun when Joshua asked for more time to win a battle?
  • It stood still
  • It turned black
  • It moved backward
  • It shone twice as bright
Joshua 10:12-13 says the sun stood still and the moon stopped until the nation took vengeance.
Question 10
Which prophet saw a valley of dry bones come to life?
  • Ezekiel
  • Daniel
  • Jeremiah
  • Hosea
Ezekiel 37 describes the vision of the dry bones being restored to life by God's Spirit.
Question 11
How many fish were left after Jesus fed the 5,000?
  • 0
  • 2
  • 5
  • 7
The fish were all used, though 12 baskets of fragments remained (Matthew 14:20).
Question 12
What miracle did Moses perform before Pharaoh to prove God's power?
  • His staff turned into a snake
  • Turning the Nile to blood
  • Bringing frogs upon Egypt
  • All of the above
Exodus 7-12 details the signs and plagues Moses performed before Pharaoh.
